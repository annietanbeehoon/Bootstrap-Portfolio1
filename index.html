About Me

A Boating Adventure by Leo Schaefer One day we were going to sail. We set out to sea but there was a storm in our way. It tore our sail and we had to use the oars to paddle to an island. Before we could get to the island, a boat got into our way. We used the horn to give them a signal of two short blasts which means ‘I want to pass you on your starboard side.’ When we reached the shore of the small island, we found twenty plants of corn. After that we repaired our sail and set off back to our home. We followed the North Star to get home. It was a fun day on Friday.
